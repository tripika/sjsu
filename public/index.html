Resources for SJSU Students

Your quick guide to student life, parking, housing, and more.

Students at San José State

San José State University sits in the heart of Silicon Valley. Students here balance academics, internships, and vibrant city life. Whether you’re a first-year student or nearing graduation, this space highlights essentials to help you thrive.

Parking at SJSU

  • Student parking permits are typically required for on-campus lots.
  • Consider off-peak parking to avoid busy morning hours.
  • VTA and micromobility can be great alternatives to driving.

Tip: Always check the latest permit policies and lot availability.

Housing Near Campus

  • Popular neighborhoods: Downtown SJ, Japantown, Spartan Keys.
  • Consider roommates to reduce costs and commute time.
  • Look for places near bus/light rail routes for convenience.

Safety first: visit listings in person and verify lease details.

International Students

  • Understand visa requirements and full-time enrollment rules.
  • Explore on-campus resources for cultural and academic support.
  • Join student orgs to build community and network.

The International Student & Scholar Services (ISSS) center offers guidance and events.
